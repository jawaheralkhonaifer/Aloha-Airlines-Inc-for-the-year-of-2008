Aloha Airlines Inc for the year of 2008

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look at : the range of the delays in minutes Depated time can have a delay, does the flight number has a usualy delay. Does the departed delay effect the arrival? Which are the origins that have the highest delays in minutes? Is there canceled flights? Which are the best places to travel from?

Dataset Overview¶

The data after cleaning contains 1418 rows. The columns contains information about the ALoha airlines unique code, day of the week, flight number, tail number, air time, arrival delay in minutes, departed delay in minutes, origin start point of the flight, destination, distance in miles, cancelled code, cancelation which determain if the trip is canceled or not, and date of the trip.
